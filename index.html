Welcome to the Darkness Overload landing page.

If you are not already familiar with this mod and it's capabilities, thats ok. to give you some idea, what we attempted to do was re-create classic versions of Riku through out the Kingdom Hearts francise, along their own unique movesets and transformations, using the Equinox framework that was previously created by Zay & Kurasa Nick.

KH III Riku

Take on the epic story of Kingdom Hearts III as Riku during the events in the Darkworld. in this specific pak, you will play as the long-haired version of Riku. when Equipped with either Soul Eater, The Way to Dawn, or KOH Keyblades, you will gain access to an entire new set of animations. each of these forms calls back to some of the earlier attacks that Riku has used through his journey with a more modern twist.This pak is intended for players who wish to play a more balanced revised version of Equinox 2.5;while many of the previous installment's attacks remain in tact, new effects and some new finishers have been added to give new comers and returning players a fresh experience.

Dark Riku

Enjoy a fast paced and powerful version of Dark Riku, using many of his Iconic attacks from both Repliku and RE:COM Dark Riku. This Pak is intended for players wish to play through the Kingdom Hearts III's story, utilizing a more up close and personal style of combat.when equipped with either Soul Eater or The Way to Dawn, you will gain access to Dark Riku's two special forms: changes: Dark Mode, and The lane Between. while in the transformed state for Dark Mode, Riku will unlock his inner darkness and unleash a flurry of devastating strikes using iconic animations from both versions of Repliku's and RE:COM's arsenal. alternatviely,while Riku is in his transformed state "The Lane Between", he gains access to his boss animations from his Hooded figure battle in KH2 as well as his traditional kh2 attacks.

KH II Riku

Learn to master balance between the light and the dark- when playing Kingdom Hearts III with this pak, players will get to play as the most recognized Riku of the series. The KH2 pak is for players who wish to dive into the action and unleash a dazzling flurry of teleports and strikes utilizing the many famous attacks directly ripped from KH2 and optimized for the battle system in Kh3

358/2 Days Riku

struggle to fight back the darkness from the depths of your heart : Days Riku is for players looking for a much more dynamic experience to combat. This pak is arguably the most unique fighting style of the rest as it uses a combination of both close ranged and long distance strikes, to obliterate your foes. Days Riku is a highly subjective play style, so mixing and matching modifiers with combo extenders will be necessary to define your own gameplay experience.
